3. **Full walkthrough** – [Create a game in BGA Studio: Complete Walkthrough](https://boardgamearena.com/doc/Create_a_game_in_BGA_Studio:_Complete_Walkthrough)
4. **Guidelines** – [BGA Studio Guidelines](https://boardgamearena.com/doc/BGA_Studio_Guidelines)
5. **Lifecycle** – [BGA game Lifecycle](https://boardgamearena.com/doc/BGA_game_Lifecycle)
6. **Pre-release** – [Pre-release checklist](https://boardgamearena.com/doc/Pre-release_checklist)
7. **Post-release** – [Post-release phase](https://boardgamearena.com/doc/Post-release_phase)
8. **Player help** – [Player Resources (Help)](https://boardgamearena.com/doc/Help) – add player help/rules to your game page
